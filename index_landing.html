Greetings!

What can you learn here?

WL is a school dedicated hobbies where organic but unusual languages are taught. From sign language to tactile signing as well as revived and whistled languages. It is even possible to learn and emulate communication in the realms of animals and insects. The question may be asked, to whom may such a school be useful? Well, not to those in pursuit of a typical hobby. The most likely hobbyist is someone who is not able to take part in a typical free time activity or simply prefers spending their time in a more unusual way.

Hobbies

Which languages?

Vernacular, whistled, endangered, extinct & revived languages.

Animals?

Bee dance, bird song and pet body language.

Art forms?

Calligraphy, choirs, painting.

Courses?

Evenings, Saturdays, weekends and on retreats.
